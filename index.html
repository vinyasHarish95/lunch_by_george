Open Doors

Lunch by George has become a social center welcoming anyone, of any gender, age, or background who needs to find a place of welcome.

Warm Food

Come in as early as 9 AM for coffee and snacks and have a healthy, hearty lunch. Open five days a week, fifty-one weeks a year.

Community Support

Many of our patrons have some form of disability, mental or physical, that prevents them from working. We are their social center, where people can find friendship and support.

Other Links

Dawn House Women's Shelter

Dawn House is a 24-hour emergency shelter offering temporary accommodation for women and children who are experiencing a housing crisis. We offer a safe, non judgemental, caring environment, counselling and support to help women find suitable accommodation.

Ryandale Shelter for the Homeless

Ryandale Shelter for the Homeless is a registered not-for-profit and charitable organization in Kingston that has been supporting the local homeless population for more than 15 years.

In From the Cold Emergency Shelter

Established in the winter of 2000, In From The Cold (IFTC) emergency shelter was opened as a temporary winter overflow shelter to assist other shelters such as Dawn House, Harbour Light, Kingston Youth Shelter and Ryandale.

Salvation Army Harbourlight

The Salvation Army Kingston Harbour Light residential dependency treatment facility was opened at it's present location on Princess Street in 1986 to address the problems of alcohol and drug abuse by men of Eastern Ontario.

Kingston Youth Shelters

The Kingston Youth Shelter is Kingston & area's only shelter specifically serving youth. We are open 24 hrs/day to assist all youth - both male and female - between the ages of 16 and 24 who are in need of emergency housing.

Martha's Table

Martha's Table is a non profit charitable organization that provides low cost nutritious meals to those in need, in safe and accepting environment.

Our mission is to support charitable programs of a humanitarian nature which benefit disadvantaged persons in our society.
