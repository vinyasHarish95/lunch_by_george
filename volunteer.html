Volunteers Are Welcomed

Please contact us if you're interested in volunteering at info@osgk.org

We especially need a truck or minivan driver to bring food from the Gardiners Road Canadian Tire to 129 Wellington between 8:30 and 10:30 on a Friday morning.

Other ways you can help:

  • donate food, clothes or household items
  • provide publicity to raise awareness about the program
  • run a fund-raising event
  • teach a skill

Our mission is to support charitable programs of a humanitarian nature which benefit disadvantaged persons in our society.
